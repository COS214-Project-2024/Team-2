\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a unit of buildings within a city that is governed by a central authority. 

\doxylink{classBuildingsUnit}{Buildings\+Unit} provides methods to manage a collection of buildings, including adding, removing, retrieving specific buildings, and managing utilities. It interacts with the \doxylink{classGovernment}{Government} class to ensure the construction and modification of buildings are within the city\textquotesingle{}s budget.
