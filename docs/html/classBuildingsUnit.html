Detailed Description

Represents a unit of buildings within a city that is governed by a central authority.

BuildingsUnit provides methods to manage a collection of buildings, including adding, removing, retrieving specific buildings, and managing utilities. It interacts with the Government class to ensure the construction and modification of buildings are within the city's budget.
